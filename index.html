    Formulae

    • Total Area = L × W × Belts
    • Theoretical Flow (L/min) = Area × 10.2; m³/h = L/min × 0.06
    • Nozzle Qty = EVEN(ROUNDUP(L/spacing) × nozzles/location × 1.05)
    • LHS (m) = EVEN(ROUNDUP(L × runs) + panel-run) × 1.1
    • Flow/Nozzle = Total L/min ÷ Nozzle Qty
    • K (calc) = (Flow/Nozzle) ÷ √Pressure
    • K Selected = 18,22,26,30,34,41,51,64,79, else 91
    • Actual Flow (L/min) = K × √Pressure × Nozzles
    • Actual Flow (m³/h) = Actual L/min × 0.06
    • Deluge Valve (mm) = Not Found if <10 or >501; else 50 if ≥10; 80 if ≥51; 100 if ≥101; 150 if ≥201
